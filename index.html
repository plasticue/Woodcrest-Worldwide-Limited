About Woodcrest Worldwide Limited

Everything for your Home & Garden in one place

We are the leading supplier of garden products to the trade. The company has built up an enviable reputation for design and innovation in solar-powered consumer products under the brands Smart Solar and Super Bright.

Additional product groups include Hanging Gardening and Plant Support under the Smart Garden brand; Clocks, Lanterns, and Doormats under Outside In designs; Garden Décor under Flamboya; Wild Birdcare under ChapelWood; Christmas products under Three Kings.

We are also delighted to be offering the Briers range of Gloves and Footwear.

We are led by our values of trust, accountability, and collaboration to serve others through this common mission.

Who Are We

We have a diverse international customer base encompassing the UK and continental Europe. Our Head Office and showroom is in London, England.

We are very committed to introducing a substantive range of new products every year, along with eye catching packaging, point of sale and merchandising, and we have a substantial bank of intellectual property covering both design and functionality.

Our aim is to give our customers a broad product choice, outstanding service, constant innovation and profitable growth.

We Serve Others
